About This Metric

Industrial Production Index (INDPRO) measures the real output of manufacturing, mining, and electric and gas utilities industries. The index is benchmarked to 2017=100 and provides a broad measure of industrial activity across the entire U.S. economy.

This broad measure reflects the overall health of U.S. industrial capacity, which is critical for defense manufacturing. Strong industrial production indicates a robust manufacturing base that can support defense contractors. It also signals the availability of skilled workers, supply chains, and industrial infrastructure.

Units: Index 2017=100
