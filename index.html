About This Dashboard

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of the defense capital landscape through four lenses:

Deal Tracker

Curated micro-level tracking of private capital investments in defense companies and projects from venture capital, private equity, and corporate sources.

Defense Investment Trends

Capital flows and new orders showing where money is moving in the defense sector and what the market expects.

Defense Industrial Health

Production capacity, output, and procurement activity measuring the health and capability of the defense industrial base.

US Industrial Health

Broader economic conditions including industrial production, business investment, credit availability, and market sentiment affecting defense manufacturing.

Data is updated regularly from public sources including Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED), financial market APIs, and curated news alerts.
